Interests

Favorite Games:

osu!

osu! is a rhythm-clicking game wherein the goal is to "click the circles to the beat". Different songs can be played in the form of beatmaps, and each song can have different and multiple beatmaps that is free for any user to download.

Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim is an open-world role-playing game where you play as the Dragonborn on a quest to defeat the World-Eater, the dragon Alduin. THe game is riddled with many side quests and the player can choose to ignore the main quest entirely.

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is an open-world role-playing game where you play as Geralt of Rivia, a witcher. In this game, his mission is to find his daughter Ciri by following evidences related to her. There are many side quests and activities along the main quest that can be done.

Dark Souls

Dark Souls is an action role-playing game focused on combat and exploration. Its main appeal is in its difficulty where it is expected for players to die multiple times. The lore of the game is not thrown directly to players but is integrated in the world and can be enjoyed by those who pay attention.

Monster Hunter: World

Monster Hunter: World is an action rpg where you play as a hunter of the fifth fleet. Your job is to hunt monsters in order to prevent further damage to the ecosystem, preserve its balance and prevent some species from going extinct.

Dark Souls 3

Dark souls 3 is an action-rpg set in the world of lothric, and you play as an Unkindled that are tasked to link the first flame and prolong the age of fire. The game is similar to the first dark souls in terms of difficulty with the difference that enemies are slightly faster and lots of improvements have been added to the gameplay such as weapon skills.

Kingdom Hearts 2

Kingdom Hearts 2 is action rpg created by Square Enix. It is the second mainline game in the series Kingdom Hearts, where you journey as the protagonist Sora, along with his friends Donald and Goofy. He ends up crossing paths the antagonists, Organization XIII.

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is an action-adventure created by the same developers who created the series of Dark Souls. You play as the "One-armed wolf" or Sekiro, a warrior bound to protect a young lord who is a descendant of an ancient bloodline. Your task is to save the young lord who has been captured.

Favorite Anime:

Kaguya-sama: Love is War

Kaguya-sama: Love is War is an anime about two people Kaguya Shinomiya and Miyuki Shirogane, who like each other but are too proud to confess their love. They instead resort to many different convoluted schemes in order to get the other person to confess.

Gundam

Gundam is a series of mecha anime which unlike its other mecha brothers, takes a more realistic approach . The mechas are explained by science, and conflicts are also realistic in nature, stemming from different real problems related to politics, economy, society.

KonoSuba: God's Blessing on This Wonderful World!

KonoSuba is an isekai anime about a protagonist named Satou Kazuma who dies and gets transported to a fantasy world with a mission of killing the Demon King terrorizing the land. Throughout their adventures, Kazuma and his party faces comical trouble and misfortune.

No Game No Life

No Game No Life is an isekai anime about the siblings Sora and Shiro who gets transported to another world where everything is ruled by games. Being very good at games, both of them traverse the world with the end-goal of beating the god Tet.

Re:Zero - Starting a New Life in Another World

Re:Zero - Starting a New Life in Another World is an isekai anime about the protagonist named Natsuki Subaru who has been transported to another world. Down the line, he realizes he has the ability to return from death and as such uses the ability to change the events that take place in the fantasy world for the better.

Sword Art Online

Sword Art Online is a science fantasy anime that takes place in the real world at 2022, The first full-dive virtual reality game has been created. After players have logged in the game, they discover that they are unable to log out. Kayaba says that players will die in real life if they die in game, and the only way to log out is to finish the 100 floors and beat SAO.

BOFURI: I Don't Want to Get Hurt, so I'll Max Out My Defense

BOFURI: I Don't Want to Get Hurt, so I'll Max Out My Defense is a Sci-Fi Fantasy anime. The protagonist is Maple who upon being urged by her friend, begins to play the VRMMORPG NewWorld Online. She puts all her stats into defense which allows her to be incredibly op and not take damage. Due to her uncanny way of playing the game, she is also able to get unconventionally op skills.

A Certain Scientific Railgun

A Certain Scientific Railgun is a futuristic Sci-Fi anime set in Academy City. The protagonist is Misaka Mikoto, a ranked 3rd Level-5 esper. The whole series focuses on the different adventures between Misaka and her friends, along with the different events which take place in Academy City, which starts light-hearted but takes a more serious turn later on.
